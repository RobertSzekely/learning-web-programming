• Why can't I see my orders?

    • Your should see all your orders under Order History. If your order doesn't show up yet, it means that the order wasn't confirmed yet.

  • How can I change the password?

    • From your Account > Settings you should find an option "Reset Password". An email with a reset link will be sent to your email address. Just click the link and follow the steps from there.

  • How to place a new order?

    • After you added at least an item to your basket, you can find a button under basket called "Order". Click the button, then select the shipping and payment methods and confirm your order.
